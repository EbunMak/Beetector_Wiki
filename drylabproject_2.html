Hardware Project:


Overview:

The Beesensor technology is composed of two main components: a sensor electrode and the diagnostic device.
The sensor electrode is composed of an integrated gold electrode. Our team will explore screen printed electrode to be integrated into the sensor. Our implementation method is inspired from iGEM 2019 NAWI Graz's beeosensor. Although screen printed electrodes are commercially available, we will explore methods to fabricate our own screen printed electrode, to allow the accessibility of the sensor in terms of cost. Using a gold SPE, Gold nanoparticle added to increase sensitivity as they act as nanoscale electrodes that electrically communicate between bioreceptors and bulk electrode materials.


Required Materials and Skills:

As mentioned above, required materials include: Three electrical connectors: Working Electorode (WE), Counter Electrode (CE), Reference Electrode (RE)
